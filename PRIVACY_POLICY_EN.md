# Privacy Policy - Al-Muadhin App

**Last Updated:** January 20, 2026

---

## Introduction

Al-Muadhin (المؤذن) is a free application for displaying prayer times and notifying users with Adhan and Iqama. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

---

## Information We Collect

### ✅ Local Information Only

Al-Muadhin app **does not collect or send any personal data to external servers**. All the following data is stored locally on your device only:

1. **Location Settings:**
   - City and country (optional)
   - Geographic coordinates (if you choose to use them)
   - Used only to calculate prayer times

2. **App Preferences:**
   - Adhan and Iqama settings
   - Volume level
   - Selected theme (light/dark)
   - Enable/disable notifications

3. **Audio Files:**
   - Custom Adhan and Iqama audio files (if any)
   - Stored locally only

---

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Use Only

- **Calculate Prayer Times:** We use your location to fetch accurate prayer times from a public API (AlAdhan API)
- **Save Preferences:** To provide a personalized experience on each use
- **Notifications:** To alert you of prayer times according to your settings

---

## Data Sharing

### ❌ We Never Share Your Data

Al-Muadhin app **does not share, sell, or transfer** any personal information to third parties. All your data remains on your device.

---

## External Services

### AlAdhan API

The app connects to [AlAdhan API](https://aladhan.com) to get prayer times:
- **What is sent:** City/country or geographic coordinates only
- **What is NOT sent:** No personal information or identifiers
- **Purpose:** To obtain accurate prayer times
- **Security:** All connections are made via encrypted HTTPS
- **AlAdhan Privacy Policy:** [https://aladhan.com/privacy-policy](https://aladhan.com/privacy-policy)

---

## Data Storage

### Local Storage

- **Location:** All settings are saved in `%USERPROFILE%\.almuadhin\config.json`
- **Security:** Simple text file that you can access and delete at any time
- **Backup:** You can manually backup or delete the file

---

## Your Rights

You have full rights to:

1. **Access:** Access all your locally saved data
2. **Modify:** Modify any settings from within the app
3. **Delete:** Delete all data by uninstalling the app or deleting the `.almuadhin` folder
4. **Control:** Disable any feature (notifications, Adhan, etc.) at any time

---

## Required Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

1. **Notifications:** To display prayer time alerts
2. **Audio Playback:** To play Adhan and Iqama
3. **Run at Startup (optional):** To start the app automatically with Windows
4. **Internet Access:** To fetch prayer times from AlAdhan API

**Note:** All permissions are optional and can be disabled from app settings or Windows.
